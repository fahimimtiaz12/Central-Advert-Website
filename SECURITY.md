# Security Policy

We take the security of our website and its users very seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability, we appreciate your help in disclosing it to us in a responsible manner.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you find a security issue, please report it to us by sending an email to our security team.

**Email:** fahimimtiaz992@gmail.com
Please include the following details in your report:
- A clear description of the vulnerability.
- Steps to reproduce the issue.
- Any relevant screenshots, logs, or proof-of-concept code.
- The potential impact of the vulnerability.

## What We Promise

- We will acknowledge receipt of your report within 48 hours.
- We will investigate your report and provide regular updates on the status.
- We will keep you informed about our progress in fixing the vulnerability.
- We will credit you for the discovery (unless you prefer to remain anonymous) in our fix release notes.

## Scope

This policy applies to security vulnerabilities found in:
- The source code of this repository.
- The live website hosted at https://centraladvert.netlify.app/
## Preferred Languages

We prefer security reports in English, but we will do our best to understand reports in other languages as well.

## Disclosure Policy

We request that you give us a reasonable amount of time to address the issue before making it public. We will work diligently to fix the vulnerability as quickly as possible.

Thank you for helping to keep our project and users safe.
